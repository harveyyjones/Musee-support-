Frequently Asked Questions

How do I connect my Spotify account?

Tap the "Connect with Spotify" button during signup or in your profile settings. You'll be redirected to authorize Vibe Match to access your Spotify data.

Why isn't my music syncing with matches?

Make sure both you and your match have:
1. Active internet connection
2. Updated to the latest app version
3. Actively listening to a song.

How does the matching algorithm work?

Our AI analyzes your listening history, favorite artists, and music preferences to find compatible matches. The more you use Spotify, the better our matches become.

How do my music preferences work?

Your preferences automatically update based on your Spotify listening activity. We analyze your listening patterns to provide the best possible matches.
